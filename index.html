Welcome to our Inventory management system

Inventory management system are the primary systems for Survey of India for inventory provided to each GDC . The Inventory management system is a organisational-level data collection system that allows the Department to collect and analyze more accurate and comprehensive information. Inventory management system provide capabilities for entering inventory records, tracking devices, and managing many other inventory-related data needs in an organisation.

Inventory Management System Supports:

 

• Handling inquiries for inventory details.

• Entering the new inventories.

• Maintaining and Updating the Inventory Details.

• Maintaining GDC details.

• Maintaining records of number of Inventories.
